## About Us

Vital Electronics Private Limited is an electronics engineering and contract manufacturing company providing turnkey solutions — from prototype to high-volume production. We operate a state-of-the-art, temperature-controlled manufacturing facility in Navi Mumbai with over 25 years of expertise.

## What We Do

#### Electronic Manufacturing Services
- PCB Assembly (SMT & Through-Hole)
- Electromechanical Assembly & Testing
- Custom Design Packaging & Box Build
- Fabricated Wire Harness Solutions
- PCB CAD Support & Design for Manufacturability (DFM)

#### Research & Development
- Custom PCB Design & Multi-layer Layout
- Embedded Firmware Development (MCU-based systems)
- Industrial Communication Protocol Integration (UART, SPI, I2C, CAN, Modbus)
- Hardware-Software Integration & Board Bring-up
- Internal Engineering & Automation Tools
